Accordion

This page is for: Content Managers Designers Developers

Accordions are similar to Tabs in that they are used to group similar pages into a single page for content heavy topics. The collapsible panels reduces page clutter and allows users to show/hide content as they please.

However, unlike Tabs, Accordions are intended for simple ‘plain text’ content, such as FAQs did you know and ‘knowledge base’ information. No images or widgets can be added to an Accordion.

Accordions are listed vertically down the page, so there are no restrictions on how many items you can list within each Accordion. You can also include more than one accordion set to a page and additional non-Accordion content can be placed after the end of an Accordion.

Example:


Collapsible Group Item #1

Content goes in here...

Collapsible Group Item #2

Content goes in here...

Collapsible Group Item #3

Content goes in here...
